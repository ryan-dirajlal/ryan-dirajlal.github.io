In-Person Safety Protocol:

Must show vaccinations at entry
KN95/N95/KF94 Masks Required
No drop-ins, please
Social distancing optional

If it's your first time back:

  • Register by email
  • Sign the waiver

Come dance with us on Fridays, September to June

The first time in person is free. After that it’s only $7 (except for special events).
Students always free, and Zoom no charge.

We Dance at Almonte Clubhouse,
105 Wisteria Way, Mill Valley, CA

Parking Instructions:
Park at one of these locations on Almonte before going upslope to Wisteria Way:
- Commuter lot on Almonte, immediately past Miller Ave. intersection
- Along fence adjacent to Tamalpais High School sports field
- Commercial lot under street lamp, avoiding reserved spots
- If needed, reserve in advance a spot at left of the Clubhouse
(email: president@kopachkafolkdancers.com)
Bring a flashlight as there are no sidewalks
Walk up the slope 200ft. to the Clubhouse, just left of the Y at Wisteria Way/Lavender Lane
Use a flashlight to walk up slope 200' to Clubhouse. Stay left of Y onto Lavender Lane
